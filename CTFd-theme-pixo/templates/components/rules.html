#Rules

    1. Team Composition:

    • Teams must consist of 3 members.
    • Each participant can only be part of one team.
    • Team members can collaborate and work together to solve challenges.

    2. Eligibility:

    • The event is open to all students of the CSE-CTIS and CTMA Departments of Jain University.
    • Participants are required to register their teams before the event starts.

    3. Conduct and Fair Play:

    • Participants are expected to maintain a high standard of sportsmanship and ethical behavior throughout the event.
    • Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or unethical behavior will result in disqualification.
    • All challenges must be solved without exploiting vulnerabilities of the CTF platform or attempting to disrupt its functionality.

    4. Challenge Solving:

    • Challenges are divided into different categories: Web Hacking, Cloud Hacking, OSINT, Cryptography, and Forensics and miscellaneous domains.
    • Teams can choose which challenges to work on and in which order.
    • Flags for each challenge should be submitted in the specified format to earn points.

    5. Flag Submission:

    • Flags must be submitted through the CTF platform using the provided submission form.
    • The format for flag submission will be provided with each challenge description. Points will be awarded for correct flag submissions.

    6. Leaderboard and Scoring:

    • The event will have a real-time leaderboard that displays team rankings based on the points earned.
    • Points are awarded for solving challenges, and the team with the highest total points will be ranked first.
    • The leaderboard will be displayed on the CTF platform and updated throughout the event.

    7. Collaboration:

    • Collaboration between different teams is strictly prohibited. Each team must work independently to solve challenges.

    8. Prizes:

    • Prizes will be awarded to the top three teams with the highest points.
    • Finalized prize details will be announced during the event.

    9. Organizers' Decision:

    • The organizers' decisions on all matters related to the event are final and binding.
    • Any dispute arising during the event will be resolved by the organizers.
